How to Use AIJobMatch

Your complete guide — read once, use forever

1
Find Jobs (Browse Tab)

Go to Find Jobs tab. Use the search box to search by job title or skill (e.g. "Python"). Filter by Location, Job Type, and Salary using the dropdowns. Click One-Click Apply on any job to submit your application instantly. Enter your User ID when prompted (get it from the Register tab).

2
Get AI Job Matches

Go to AI Match tab. Type your skills in the box (e.g. Python, Machine Learning, SQL) or click the Quick Add buttons. Select your experience level and click Get AI Recommendations. The AI ranks all 42+ jobs by how well they match your skills and shows a % match score. Click Skill Gap on any result to see exactly what skills you're missing for that role.

3
Upload Your Resume

Go to Resume tab. Click the upload box to open your File Manager and select your resume saved as a .txt file. You can also drag and drop the file. Or just paste your resume text in the text box below. Click Extract Skills — the AI will find all your tech skills automatically. Then click Use for AI Matching to instantly run recommendations with those skills.

Tip: To save your resume as .txt on Windows: Open your resume in Notepad → File → Save As → change file type to "Text Document (.txt)"
4
Set Job Alerts

Go to Alerts tab. Enter your User ID, type keywords like Python, Data Science and an optional location. Click Set Alert. You'll get a notification whenever matching jobs are posted. You can delete alerts anytime from the same page.

5
Track Applications

Go to Applied tab. Enter your User ID and click Load to see all your submitted applications with their current status: Applied → Reviewing → Shortlisted → Hired.

For Employers

E
Post Jobs & Manage Applicants

Click the icon in the top-right to switch to Employer Mode. Register an account with role = Employer. Use Post Job to create a listing with salary, location, and required skills. View My Postings to see all your jobs, then click Applicants to review candidates and update their status to Shortlisted, Rejected, or Hired.

What is my User ID?

Your User ID is the number shown after you register. It's used in Applied, Alerts, and History tabs to load your personal data.
If you forgot it — re-register with the same email and note the ID shown. The ID is also printed in the success message when you first register.
